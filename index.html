About Us

TenSketch is revolutionary IT start-up founded on 26th August 2017 by two young and passionate, Balachandiran and Bhuvanesh form Chennai. They founded this company just a week after they met each other in a start-up meet in Chennai. Both found each other’s values so much alike and they had a burning desire to take up Entrepreneurship as a major tool to solve challenges of society and business world. They both have a diverse skillset in the field of IT, Management, Marketing, Human Resources, etc. They joined hands together with a major goal to provide Holistic web design solutions to all levels of business and society. They started with web designing services for now, and planned to expand the other verticals of software and technology
